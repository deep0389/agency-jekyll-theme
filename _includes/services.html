Tamasha Workshop

The workshops have been designed in collaboration with national school of drama alumunus to make the participants production ready. It is to ensure that we are able to train the actors to reach a level of excellence. These workshops give direct entry into the tamasha productions circuit be it Films or theatre. The current workshop is going on with Vivek Ram kanojia as an instructor. He is a 2014 pass out of NSD and his play Welcome Zindagi was awarded the best play in the year 2016.

Tamasha Corporate

We have a wide range of corporate shows ready. The shows can be designed depending on the requirement of the corporate. Our show Park written by Manav kaul has been a hit in various companies for its hilarious satirical take on our society. It was last performed for EDU3 in Radisson Hotel Dwarka.

Tamasha Kids

We strongly believe that the way to incorporate theatre and performing arts into the society is to cater to children. Tamasha kids is a kid oriented workshop that gives children an early insight into the world of theatre and performing arts. The course is handmade by professionals to ensure that the children have a great experience.
